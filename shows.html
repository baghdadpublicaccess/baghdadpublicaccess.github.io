BTV Shows

All shows are produced with equipment provided by Baghdad Public Access and are exclusive to BTV.

The Baghdad Talent Show - Hosted by Donnie Coleman

Baghdad residents show off their talents.

Tornado Watch with Chuck - Hosted by Chuck Sanders

Chuck Sanders reviews Tornado footage, and does a little stormchasing of his own.

The Gary Langdon Show - Hosted by Gary Langdon

Gary Langdon covers local news and interviews people for his late-night variety talk show.

Good Morning Baghdad - Hosted by Cheryl Lindon

Start the day off right with Cheryl Lindon's morning program. Good Morning Baghdad covers the weather, national news, and local special-interest stories.

D.I.Y. Karate with Sensei Smith - Hosted by Randall Smith

Learn karate and other forms of self defense with former accountant Sensei Randall Smith.

The Home Gardening Hour - Hosted by Ruth Warner

Ruth Warner and her friends discuss gardening techniques in this relaxing show.

Full Contact Golf - Rec League - Hosted by BTV Sports Commentator Phil Evans

Phil Evans covers the intense and exciting matches of Baghdad's Full Contact Golf recreational league.
